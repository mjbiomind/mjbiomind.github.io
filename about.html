Mary Jacobs

A bilingual biostatistics and health data science professional connecting clinical research, statistical reasoning, and computational methods to improve mental health research.

Profile

Mary Jacobs is based in San Francisco and has supported research across psychiatry, neurology, and large-scale educational assessment at UCSF. Her experience includes REDCap, Epic/APeX, OnCore, clinical research operations, data quality, bilingual participant-facing work, and reproducible analysis.

Her developing research program centers on causal inference, clinical NLP, survival analysis, and interpretable predictive modeling for trauma, psychiatric outcomes, and health disparities, particularly among Spanish-speaking and historically underrepresented communities.

Her long-term objective is doctoral-level biomedical data science research that converts complex clinical data into precise, ethical, and socially meaningful decisions.
