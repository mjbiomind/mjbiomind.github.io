Research

Methods with human consequence.

A research direction focused on trauma, physiology, social conditions, psychiatric outcomes, and equitable clinical decision-making.

Central Question

How can statistical learning identify modifiable pathways between trauma, physiology, social conditions, and long-term mental health outcomes?

Research Interests

  • Causal Inference

    Observational health-data methods for estimating effects while making assumptions, confounding structures, and uncertainty explicit.

  • Clinical NLP

    Extraction of psychiatric symptoms, social context, temporality, and clinical meaning from unstructured records.

  • Computational Psychiatry

    Interpretable models of heterogeneous pathways in PTSD, depression, chronic stress, and recovery.

  • Mental Health Equity

    Research designed for Spanish-speaking and historically underrepresented communities, with attention to measurement validity and deployment risk.
